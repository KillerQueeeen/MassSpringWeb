Interactive Diff-Tachi

Instructions

1. Draw your own spring robot in the canvas below.
2. Hold "d" and click on a point to delete it.
2. Click on "ToggleGrid" button to hide the grid in canvas.
3. Under "Grid" mode, hold "b" to draw a predefined square.
4. Click on "Train" button to train the robot to walk.
5. Press left/right arrow to let your robot walk.
